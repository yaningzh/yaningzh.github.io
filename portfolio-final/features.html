Introduction

Sichuan cuisine is characterized by numb, spicy, fragrant, fresh, oily, rich in flavor, and put three kinds of peppers in important position (chili, Sichuan peppercorn and black pepper). The reason of forming this kind of taste is because of the topography of Sichuan, it has lower altitude and surrounded by mountains, because of this, Sichuan always heavy humidity and by having spicy food, people can avoid of getting rheumatism when they are old.

Fish-flavored

Make use of onion, ginger, garlic pickled pepper, Sichuan bean paste, Sugar, Vinegar and soy sauce. Famous dishes include fish-flavored shredded pork, fish-flavored eggplant, etc.

Spicy

Make use of all kinds of chilis and always a lot of oils. Famous dishes include hotpot, spicy chicken, etc.

Pepper flavor

Make use of sichuan peppercorn and black pepper which will let your tongue feels numb. Famous dishes include pepper belly slices, etc.

Hot and sour

Make use of all kinds of chilis and sauerkraut which has pickled for a long time. Famous dishes include fish with pickled mustard greens, etc.
