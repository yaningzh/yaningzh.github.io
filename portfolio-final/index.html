Sichuan Cuisine

Sichuan, a place where I spent my four years undergrad there, I am so impressed with the food there. The first time I went to Sichuan is when I graduate from middle school, mom and I went there to play, at that time I was not used to the food there, but after a few months when I went to college, I was so impressed by the food there and always dine together with friends of ordering Sichuan Cuisine. Right now, I am at abroad, I still miss the decilious food there.

Sichuan cuisine is one the eight main cuisine in China. The main taste of it is spicy and hemp, it always use heavy oil and heavy chilli. It has a lot of delicious food which attracts people all over the world to have a try on that. This page will introduce this cuisine to you. Sichuan is in the southwest of China, it is surrounded by mountains, the terrain of Sichuan is the basin and it is extremely wet all year round, people eat spicy food in order to keep them heatlth.
